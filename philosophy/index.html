Children grow best simply by being at the center of their journey.

We embrace curiosity of every creative child.

We respect that the child is the co-constructor of knowledge. This means that the children's interests and their innate curiosity help choreograph our child-centered, nature-based curriculum.

We teach from the greatest childcare thinkers and practices.

We support the children's exploration of their environment for learning and the provocation to take another look. Our philosophy combines some of the teachings of the greatest educators in early childhood: Jean Piaget, Bev Bos, Becky Bailey and Reggio Emilia's Loris Malaguzzi.

We lead with creativity and trust with each child everyday.
